> These newer styles of vinyasa yoga are a less structured offshoot of the [Ashtanga Yoga](https://www.yogabasics.com/learn/ashtanga-yoga/) system. Ashtanga Yoga has a strict structure and precise set of rules and was developed in by Sri K. Pattabhi Jois. His teacher, [Krishnamacharya](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tirumalai_Krishnamacharya), is attributed with being the originator of the Vinyasa concept in yoga.
