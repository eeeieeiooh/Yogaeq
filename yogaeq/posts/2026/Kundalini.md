### Kundalini - (Raja Yoga), Kingly Yoga

- Hatha - physical strength. build up a strong body. prereq. to raja, because raja demands a lot.
- Kundalini: The Coiled up One
- Energy of the spirit. 
- The goal of this yoga is lacking the animation of this spiritual power in most of our lives. there are centers on the way up. the spine is shown as a tree with seven centers. 
- Imaged as a feminine serpent: kundalini 


What has become known as "Kundalini yoga" in the 20th century, after a technical term particular to this tradition, is actually a synthesis of Bhakti Yoga (devotion and chanting), Raja Yoga (meditation) and Shakti Yoga (the expression of power and energy)
