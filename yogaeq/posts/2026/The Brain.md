CIA Declassified Document 2001 Illustration of the Right and Left Hemispheres of the Brain. - The strict division shown in that CIA document is outdated

While some functions show lateralization (language tends toward left, some spatial processing toward right), it's not the clean split popularized in the 1970s-80s.
