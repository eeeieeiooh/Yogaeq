# **Spirituality**

An individual practice that involves exploring universal themes like love, compassion, and wisdom. It's often connected to religion, but it's also becoming more connected to philosophy. Spirituality is based on an emotional approach to seeking truth, and it's directly connected to the soul. 
# **Philosophy**

The study of fundamental truths about the world, life, and our relationships to each other and the world. It's a thought process that involves critically examining beliefs and looking for rational justifications for them. The term "philosophy" literally means "love of wisdom"
