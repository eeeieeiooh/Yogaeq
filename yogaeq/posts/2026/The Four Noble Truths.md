# The Four Noble Truths

1. **Dukkha** - Suffering exists: Life involves dissatisfaction, pain, and impermanence
2. **Samudaya** - Suffering has a cause: Craving, attachment, and ignorance create suffering
3. **Nirodha** - Suffering can end: Liberation (nirvana) and peace are possible through letting go
4. **Magga** - There is a path to end suffering: Follow the Noble Eightfold Path of wisdom, ethics, and mental discipline
